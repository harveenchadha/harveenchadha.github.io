05 Patents

Intellectual property filed at Microsoft, focused on speech translation.

USPTO · 19/038,512 2026

Length-Aware Speech Translation for Edge Video Dubbing

H.S. Chadha, A.S. Subramanian, S. Bansal, V. Joshi, R.R. Mehta, J. Xue

An on-device (edge) speech translation system for video dubbing that generates translations matching the duration of the original speech. Introduces a phoneme-based Length Sensitive Speech Translation (LSST) model that produces short, normal, and long translation variants, along with Length Aware Beam Search (LABS) for efficient single-pass decoding. Enables seamless audio-video synchronization without cloud dependency.

USPTO · 18/587,660 2025

Translating Speech in a Gender-Aware Manner

S. Bansal, V. Joshi, R. Dsouza, R.R. Mehta, H.S. Chadha, A. Mukherjee

A method for speech translation systems to produce gender-appropriate translations by leveraging speaker gender cues directly from audio signals, eliminating the need for explicit gender input. Employs LLMs to rectify training data translations based on speaker gender, then fine-tunes the speech translation model to produce gender-specific output. Proposes a three-mode fine-tuned model for different gender inference scenarios. Demonstrates a 70% improvement in translations for female speakers compared to baselines like Seamless M4T and Canary on the MuST-SHE test set.
