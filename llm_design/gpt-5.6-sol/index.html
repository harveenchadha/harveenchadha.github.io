01 Profile

A path from systems engineering to frontier AI.

An AI researcher focused on making advanced models useful across India's 121 languages and more than a billion speakers.

At ThoughtWorks, created Vakyansh, an open-source speech recognition toolkit that became part of India's Bhashini platform. At Microsoft, developed speech recognition and translation systems for Indic languages.

Current work at Sarvam AI covers data curation, supervised fine-tuning, evaluation, and search infrastructure for India-first large language models.
